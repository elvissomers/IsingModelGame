Tutorial

Rules:

  • The game has Arrows and Secret Blocks (+ or -).
  • A Green Block (+) is happy when its two Arrows point the SAME way.
  • A Red Block (-) is happy when its two Arrows point DIFFERENT ways.
  • Happy blocks give 0 Energy. Unhappy blocks add 1 to your Energy!
  • Your goal is to guess all the secret blocks by tracking your Energy!

Hints:

  1. Make all Arrows point the same way to count the Red Blocks (-).
  2. Try changing just one Arrow at a time to see how the Energy changes!
